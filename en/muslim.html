Muslim culture promotion program

Muslim culture promotion program

The Muslim culture promotion program at the Center for Muslim Culture in Warsaw aims to introduce the public to the richness of Islamic heritage, its history, art and traditions.

Through various initiatives, we want to build understanding, openness and mutual respect.

As part of the program we are implementing:

  • Thematic exhibitions presenting Muslim art and culture
  • Author's meetings and lectures on the history and present day of the Islamic world
  • Festivals and cultural events to promote the diversity of Muslim traditions
  • Film projections and discussions on topics related to Islamic culture
  • Cooperation with cultural institutions and social organizations

We invite all interested parties to participate in our events to explore and learn about the richness of Muslim culture together.

The center is also a meeting place for the Muslim community, offering a space for prayer, reflection and integration.

We invite everyone who is interested in learning about Islam and building bridges between cultures to participate in our initiatives.

You are warmly invited to visit the Muslim Cultural Center in Warsaw - a place where dialogue, knowledge and mutual respect bring together people of different traditions and worldviews.
