Plan your visit

How to schedule a visit

  • Opening hours: The center is open to the public at certain times - see our website for up-to-date information.
  • Individual tour:Requires registration - contact us.
  • Group tours: For organized groups, we offer the possibility of booking a guided visit.
    Please contact us in advance to arrange the date and program of the tour.
  • Reservations: To book an appointment, contact us by phone or email - see the “Contact Us” tab for contact information.

What do we offer to visitors?

  • Permanent and temporary exhibitions presenting Muslim culture.
  • Educational workshops and thematic meetings.
  • Opportunity to participate in lectures, debates and cultural events.
  • A space for reflection and intercultural dialogue.

You are warmly invited to visit the Muslim Cultural Center in Warsaw - a place where dialogue, knowledge and mutual respect bring together people of different traditions and worldviews.
